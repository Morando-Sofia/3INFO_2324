Instruction

Preparation:

  • As a group, chose one of the art
    movements that we discussed and
    choose artwork to discuss.

Activity:

  • In the first box, paste your chosen
    artwork.
  • Look at the artwork then answer the
    following questions as a group.

1 min:

  • Write tour names on the stiky notes.
  • Use them to answer the questions.

10 mins:

  • Write down your answers to the
    questions in the boxes.
  • These can just be phrases or bullet
    points.

5 mins:

  • As a group, discuss the answers.
  • Decided which answersyou will present
    to the class.
